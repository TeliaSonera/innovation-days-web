Frequently asked questions

The webpage is in English. Is this a global Telia event?

No, this is an event only for Norway. We use English because we want to include our English-speaking colleagues. Most of the internal communication will be in Norwegian, and the presentations will be in Norwegian.

Is this event for techies?

This event is for everyone that works in Telia Norway AS, we want attendees from different parts of the organization, who can bring their specific knowledge and skills to the event.

I don’t have an idea.. What do I do?

Do not worry! There will be other attendees with ideas, and we need people that can help develop the ideas. That can be you :-)

Which prerequisites are needed?

None, specifically. If you work in Telia Norway AS you already have what it takes. Your point of view is important and it can be used to develop great ideas. Just be open-minded, enthusiastic, dare, share and create. But remember this is a competition! Bring your A-game;)

I may have an idea but I am not sure if it is any good…

First step, Dare! Do not let your doubts stop you from submitting your idea. Typically at these kind of events the idea you first introduce will at the course of the event changed, evolved and become better because you work in a team that has important and valid feedback.

Second step: talk to a possible costumer or user of the idea. Feedback from actual users is invaluable!

If that did not make things clearer, send us an e-mail, we will gladly help you out.

What ideas can I submit?

The ideas can be about anything related to Telia’s ecosystem affecting our customers, colleagues, partners, future business areas.

Please read the criteria for the submitted ideas.
